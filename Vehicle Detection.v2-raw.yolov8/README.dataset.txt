Images are from an **open cctv** in [link](https://cctv.jogjakota.go.id) at **Simpang APPI 2 at Jl. Mentri Supeno**.
Images **was not HD** because it's from the resource itself (cctv).
There are **1000 images** with **750 images** for **training**, **150 images** for **testing**, and **100 images** for **validation**.
Vehicle split into **4 type**:
- **car** (which include *pickup car* type)
- **motorbike** (*cart wasn't include* if it was attached)
- **truck** (which include *pickup box* type)
- **bus** (which include *minibus* like *elf car* type)
